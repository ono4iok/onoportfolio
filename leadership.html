Leadership

Onofiok has been taken up leadership positions in various community development endeavors and has demonstrated a commitment to the SDG goals. Onofiok was the president of a Drug-free and Quality development service group where he led 69 people on drug abuse sensitizations across Uvwie Local Government area of Delta State, Nigeria. This led to him being awarded a Certificate of leadership by the National Enforcement Agency, Delta State Command. While at Griot Studios, Onofiok has also carried out film screenings and marches promoting gender equality across the 6 geo-political zones of Nigeria. Onofiok is also an alumni of the Young African Leaders Initiative (YALI) where he served as a graphic designer, video editor and front-end website developer for Emandes Social Development Network and Brainbox Studios. Onofiok is currently enrolled in leadership school with the African Centre for Leadership, Strategy and Development (Centre LSD).
